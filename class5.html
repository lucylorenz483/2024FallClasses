Personal Financial Planning

In this class I will learn how to understand the importance of personal financial planning so I can live a better life (economic well-being and financial security). Be able to use financial statements and ratios used to evaluate my current financial situation. I will learn how to identify potential risk management needs and the need for employer-provided and/or independently purchased insurance protection. This class will also understand to discuss the importance of income tax planning and implications for financial decisions and understand the use of cash management, credit, and debt strategies to reach my goals.
